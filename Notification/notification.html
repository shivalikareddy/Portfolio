A short film exploring practical and natural lighting.

Role

Direction, Cinematography, Editing

Timeline

2 Days

Tools

Premiere Pro

Collaborators

Independent

CONCEPT

This short film follows a college student through an ordinary Thursday, using only natural and practical light to shape the narrative. Shot on a single camera with no added lighting, every source in the frame comes from the built environment, including desk lamps, hallway fixtures, screens, streetlights, and sunlight. The project explores how mood and emotion can be conveyed through minimal equipment and ambient conditions alone. Influenced by the framing and color sensibilities of Wes Anderson, the story builds quietly toward a small but meaningful shift when a long-awaited notification transforms an otherwise muted day.

EQUIPMENT USED

  • Canon EOS R8
  • Tripod Stand
  • Sekonic L-308X-U Analog Light meter
