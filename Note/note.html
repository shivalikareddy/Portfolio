NOTE

A short film exploring lens techniques and visual storytelling.

Role

Direction and Cinematography

Timeline

3 Days

Tools

Premiere Pro, After Effects

Collaborators

Katherine Costal, Alessandra Perreras, Cindy Lo

CONCEPT

This short film explores visual storytelling through lens variation, composition, and controlled lighting rather than dialogue. The narrative centers on two people meeting at a café, with emotion conveyed through body language, pacing, and environmental detail instead of spoken words. Designed as an experiment in lens techniques, the project integrates telephoto, macro, and wide-angle perspectives within a single sequence to shape mood and spatial perception. Shot on the Blackmagic Pocket Cinema Camera 6K, the approach focused on shaping available light and adapting to location, allowing framing, compression, and depth to drive the emotional tone of the encounter.

  • Canon EF 100 mm f/2.8 Macro Lens
  • Portable Light Stand
  • Small Table sized Light Stand
